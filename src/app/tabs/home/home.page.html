Welcome to Visa Appointment Scheduler

This application helps you easily schedule, manage, and track your visa appointments. Whether you are applying for a student, work, or tourist visa, our app provides a streamlined and user-friendly experience.

Use the tabs below to navigate through the app:

  • Home: View this introductory information.
  • Appointments: Schedule new appointments and view your booked appointments.
  • Profile: Manage your profile and log out of the application.

If you have any questions or need assistance, feel free to contact our support team.
